How do I report a lost item?

Report your lost items by filling out the Lost Items form in the above section in the navigation bar, then clicking report lost item.

❓

What should I do if I find someone's item?

Report it as soon as possible in the found items, report found item section above, then bring the item down to the security office at your earliest convenience, or otherwise you will be contacted if the owner is found before then.

❓

How long are items kept before being donated or disposed?

Perishable items are discarded within 3-7 days and valuables are kept for up to a year before discarded or donated.
